Reviews and Feedbacks
This workflow manages the community's preferences through reviews.

1. After visiting a restaurant, a logged-in user submits a review consisting of a 1–5 star rating and feedback. 
2. This rating will be recorded and the average rating will be displayed up to date.
3. Moderation: If a review is flagged, the Admin can hide or show review from the admin dashboard.
4. According to the Admin’s action, the reviews would update in real time.
